**Some Links, Tips & Thoughts for the day**
**Career Stuff**
  I got a very interesting email yesterday entitled "GitHub is not your Resume" it was from Charles Max Wood from the
  excellent ng-newsletter and devchat.tv two resource that I really recommend. In the email the points are that having
  a great GitHub profile is a supplement to not a replacement for a great resume/cv. I do agree with the point but looking
  at some of the job adverts now and the list of skills needed sometimes using side projects is the only way of demonstrating
  these skills, GitHub being a skill in and of itself. I love GitHub and have lots of projects and have had job offers come
  my way from doing practical projects using hot technologies, I suspect with the rise of machine learning and related this
  maybe something which happens a lot more in the future.
